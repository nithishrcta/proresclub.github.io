Our Objectives and Mission

  1. Developing product based research culture among students of Reva university.
  2. Bridging the gap between theoretical and reality by encouraging research culture among students.
  3. Embracing the product culture and building great startups from it
  4. Creating a competitive spirit among students by organising Idea Hackathon Competition
  5. Bringing industry icons and leaders to Reva University and organising Talks with great minds to introduce the reality to industry to students
  6. Encouraging students to take up innovative and engineering challenges to solve real world problems.
